Partner With Us

Join leading organizations that collaborate with CMU's Computer Vision group to advance the frontiers of visual AI.

The Computer Vision group at Carnegie Mellon University brings together world-class researchers working on 3D vision, generative models, computational imaging, and robotics. Corporate sponsorship enables us to pursue ambitious research agendas while providing partners with unique access to cutting-edge discoveries and top talent.
