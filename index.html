Computer Vision @ CMU

Pioneering research in visual perception, 3D understanding, generative models, and embodied AI at Carnegie Mellon University.

Research Areas

📐

3D Vision & Reconstruction

Shape prediction, neural radiance fields, multi-view geometry, and 3D scene understanding from images and video.

🎨

Generative Models

Diffusion models, GANs, image synthesis, text-to-image generation, and creative visual content creation.

📷

Computational Imaging

Novel camera systems, physics-based vision, time-of-flight imaging, and computational photography.

🤖

Robotics & Embodied Vision

Robot learning, autonomous driving, manipulation, embodied AI, and visual reasoning for robotic systems.
